CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation)

  • Check for responsiveness.
  • Call emergency services (112/108).
  • Begin 30 chest compressions at 100–120 bpm.
  • Give 2 rescue breaths.
  • Repeat until help arrives.

Choking

  • Ask: "Are you choking?"
  • Encourage coughing if mild.
  • If severe: Give 5 back blows + 5 abdominal thrusts.
  • Repeat until object is expelled or person becomes unconscious.

Burns

  • Cool the burn under running water for 10-15 minutes.
  • Do not apply ice or creams.
  • Cover with clean cloth or cling film.
  • Seek medical help if severe.

Emergency Numbers (India)

  • 🚨 General Emergency: 112
  • 🚑 Ambulance: 108
  • 🚒 Fire: 101
